Agatha

Agatha is a member of the Indigo Elite Four in either the Kanto or Johto region.

She isn't a gym leader and is the oldest member of the Elite Four to date.

She made her game debut in Pokémon Origins and her anime debut in The Scheme Team.

She began as a member of the Kanto Elite Four.
