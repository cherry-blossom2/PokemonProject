Bug

Characteristics

  • Bug pokemon grow fast, which is shown by their fast evolution time.
  • The bug type is supposed to represent the beauty of nature.
  • Bug types live among the forests.
  • They are the 6th most common type, with 9.4% of pokemon being bug types (as of the 8th generation).

Burgh

Burgh appears in Pokemon Black And White as the leader of the Bug Gym.

He gives the Insect badge and the TM "Struggle Bug" to the player that defeats him.

Burgh's gym is in Castelia, and is the third gym to be challenged by the player.
